About The Author

"I, Arun Joseph Varghese, am a Healthcare IT guy, who by happenstance has chosen to tread through opportunities of how healthcare providers can be revolutionized by the same wave which has hit the BFSI, Telecom, Retail and other sectors. I am a Bioinformatics Engineering graduate from the VIT (Vellore Institute of Technology) University, an alumnus from the Institute of Health Management Research, Bangalore as a Post Graduate in Hospital Management (PGPHM) and an MBA in International Hospital and Healthcare Management Research from the Frankfurt School of Finance and Management (in collaboration with HEC Montreal). I have the experience of having worked formerly with Tata Consultancy Services and is currently employed as the Product and Marketing Manager at Insta Health Solutions Pvt. Ltd."
