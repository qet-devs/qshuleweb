  • Is your clinic / hospital geared up for Malaffi Integration?

    • Malaffi is an innovative, unified health information exchange platform for health care providers in Abu Dhabi region that facilitates a more patient centric approach to health care provision. Malaffi enables healthcare facilities, health care professionals and government authorities across the emirates of Abu Dhabi to access and share patient’s medical information in order to deliver better healthcare quality and enhance patient safety and overall health outcomes.
    Through the provider portal of Malaffi the health care professionals will have access to patient’s full medical history such as
    • Medication List
    • Diagnosis list
    • Allergies list
    • Procedures List
    • Clinical encounter history and notes
    • Laboratory and Radiology results

  • Is the patient data safe and secure?

    • Malaffi follows high information security standards. The clinical data access will be given based on the user’s role within the health care organization and oversight of appropriate use of Malaffi is a shared responsibility between DOH, Health care facilities and Malaffi.

  • How to proceed with Malaffi Integration?

    • To complete the Malaffi integration as a health care provider you need to communicate with Malaffi team and sign up as a participant. The health care provider needs to communicate with their software provider and enable them to complete the integration with Malaffi.

  • How Insta HMS by Practo supports Malaffi Integration?

    • Practo as an ISO 27001 certified organization on security has taken cautious measures on Patient data safety and already started developments towards Malaffi integration. With close to 100 health care facilities using Insta HMS® across emirates of Abu Dhabi we are working directly with Malaffi team towards the integration. We will be achieving SSO integration too with Malaffi so that the health care professionals can access the patient information from the health exchange inside Insta HMS without moving out of EMR window. Practo is very much proud to be part of this great initiative by DOH.

  • Migration to Insta HMS

    • We are welcoming the health care providers in Abu Dhabi region to migrate to Insta HMS® to have a seamless integration experience with Malaffi. Migrating to Insta HMS® will provide various benefits to the health care providers on the following and not limited to Increase in Revenue, Enhanced Patient experience, Hassle free Insurance Claim management, Specialty EMR documentation etc.

    Please click here to enroll yourself for the change or write to malaffi@practo.com
